About Me

I am a minimalist developer and I love to create beautiful, accessible, responsive websites that meet my client's needs. My design centers on performance, responsiveness and user accessibility. My goal is to bridge design and emerging technology and I always look forward to convert ideas into realities.

Currently, I'm focused on design systems, accessibility, and performance optimisation. I specialise in React, TypeScript, and modern CSS, with a growing interest in AI-powered development — using prompt engineering and LLM APIs to extend what's possible in the products I build.

Beyond the screen, I'm interested in music, art direction, and the intersection of design and engineering. I believe great work comes from the details.
